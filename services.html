Our Services

At Prime Safaris, we offer a range of services to make your Safari experience unforgettable. Our services include:

  • Guided Safaris: Join our expert guides on a trailing safari adventure through Africa's most incredible wildlife reserves.
  • Safari Camps: relax in our luxurious Safari camps, where you will enjoy comfortable accommodations and standing views.
  • Conservation projects: participate in our conservation projects and make a difference in the lives of endangered species.
  • Customize Safaris: who will work with you to create a customized safari experience tailored to your needs and interests.
  • Wildlife Photography: join our Wildlife Photography Safaris Capture the beauty of Africa's incredible wildlife.
  • Safari Tours: Our guided tours take you through Africa's most iconic wildlife reserves and national parks.
  • Adventure Activities: participate in adventure activities such as hiking, bird watching, or hot air balloon rides.
  • Cultural experiences: experience the rich culture of Africa through visits to local villages and interruptions with indigenous communities.
